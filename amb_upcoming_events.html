8th Annual Charity Golf Tournament

Friday, October 24, 2014

Admiral Baker Golf Course

Join us for our Annual Golf Tournament benefitting the Ambassadors Foundation. This year our emphasis is on FUN! We will be playing in a light hearted 4-person scramble. This format allows all players to play from the best shot on their team. Giving avid golfers the chance to shine, while newer players enjoy a faster and less challenging round of golf. Take advantage of this opportunity to bond with your teammates in an entertaining and exciting atmosphere.
