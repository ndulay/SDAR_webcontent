Gain expert insight in navigating today's economic real estate environment by attending SDAR's 5th Annual Regional Real Estate Summit. Sign up by August 12 and receive a member-only early-bird price.

This year's summit will feature a panel of experts spanning multiple segments of the industry that will share their perspectives on the market and what the opportunities and challenges are in today's economic climate. Become better informed about the economic state of real estate in California and how this can affect your industry and your business. Gain insight from respected experts about market trends and forecasts to allow you to pilot your business in the ever-changing market.
