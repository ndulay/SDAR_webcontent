The real estate industry offers many designations, certifications, and accreditations in light of the abundance of market segments (buyer, seller, residential, commercial, property management, seniors, short sales, etc.) (CalBRE Sponsor #0282)

These courses are designed to increase your experience and expertise upon completion of all required courses. Many of the courses offer BRE course credit.

Not all designation/certification courses are NAR-approved, but are offered for professional development and to help you stand out amongst the competition.
