Broker Circle Held Monthly at Various SDAR Service Centers – For Brokers Only

SDAR will host free Broker Circle mixer events each month at our various service centers throughout the county. Gather and exchange best business practices, ideas, information, and lessons learned. Register online and learn more details, as well as upcoming dates and locations.
