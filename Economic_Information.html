{mooblock=National Association of REALTORS® (NAR)}

The National Association of REALTORS® releases national and regional existing-home sales price and volume statistics on or about the 25th of each month. Each report includes data for 12 months and annual totals going back three years.

National and Regional Housing Statistics

{/mooblock}

{mooblock=California Association of REALTORS® (C.A.R.)}

The California Association of REALTORS® pulls sales data from a survey of more than 90 associations of REALTORS® throughout the state, and represent statistics of existing single-family detached homes only.
