Veterans

San Diego is home to the third-largest veteran population in the United States. San Diego is also known as America's Finest City, and SDAR's leadership wants that to be the case for our veterans and active-duty services members living here.

SDAR works hard to ease struggles for veterans, active-duty military members and their families. We do this in myriad ways, particularly through our Ambassadors Foundation and such programs as first-time home buyer's workshops and VA loan education.
