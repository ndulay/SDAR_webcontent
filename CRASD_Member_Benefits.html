Membership in Commercial Real Estate Alliance of San Diego (CRA San Diego) is reserved for commercial real estate practitioners who conduct their business successfully and ethically. CRA San Diego provides education and information about current commercial real estate issues and trends, assisting its members to effectively compete in the marketplace while offering the highest level of service to their clients.

Serving as an umbrella organization locally, CRA San Diego promotes programs and educational offerings of CAR, NAR, CCIM, SIOR, CPM, ARM and CRE.

One of thirty-four commercial associations of REALTORS® nationwide, CRA San Diego is where resources and opportunities meet!

REALTOR® Level Membership

Joining CRA San Diego at the REALTOR® Member Level offers additional benefits. As a REALTOR® member, you have access to the National Association of REALTORS® (NAR) Alliance Program and the CALIFORNIA ASSOCIATION OF REALTORS® (C.A.R) Members Advantage Program.

Enjoy the added benefits of negotiated discounts with the CAR Members Advantage Program Partners

Health Insurance Benefits Guaranteed!

When you join CRA San Diego at the REALTOR® Member Level, you are guaranteed eligibility for health, dental and vision insurance. For further information, please visit the CALIFORNIA ASSOCIATION OF REALTORS® website: www.car.org.
