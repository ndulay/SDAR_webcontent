Navigating the Multi-Screen Society:

Description: SDAR's new Strategic Partner, Barcode Realty, will be hosting this webinar to help SDAR members better understand how consumers and Realtors use devices in different ways. The United States has become a society of multi-screen consumers. We split our day between smartphones, tablets, e-book readers, computers and televisions.
