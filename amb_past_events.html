3rd Annual Holiday House Charity Drive December 6, 2013

This year's Holiday House celebration was held at a stunning 8,000+ sq ft home in Rancho Santa Fe. Over 200 local REALTORS® and supporters joined us as we filled a home with donations for local children, military families, seniors, and homeless.

The evening featured an appearance by Miss San Diego and Miss Monterey, as well as food, drinks, and live music! Thank you to everyone who attended, and a special thanks to our charity representatives who shared their touching stories.
