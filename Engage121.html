What is Engage121?

Engage121 enables organizations to monitor, evaluate and respond in real time to social media activity. SDAR brokerage companies now have the ability to work cooperatively with local offices on the development of social media communities.

Whether a broker or agent, Engage121 gives you compelling industry news and local stories you can post in minutes to your Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn accounts. A daily 'Hot Content' email offers five suggested stories to post from your phone, tablet or computer. Engage all your social contacts on one convenient page, and position yourself as the local real estate expert.

What are the benefits of Engage121?

Whether you are a real estate agent promoting your online presence, or a brokerage with dozens of franchise offices, the Engage121 social media software enables you to create a one-to-one relationship with your customers, influence your key business metrics, and drive traffic to outlets, branches, stores.

Key member benefits include:

  • A social media dashboard to manage multiple social networks, with content deployment in one centralized, controlled and compliant manner
  • Respond in real time with one-click posts to all your social media platforms
  • Manage social media with ease, efficiency and creativity
  • Scheduled calendar of Facebook messages and tweets
  • Daily emails with suggested content (articles and news) for users to post to their networks
  • Analyze and track social media traffic and analytics
  • Measure your reach in the social network
  • Increase traffic to branch offices
  • Efficiently manage content creation and workflow
  • Easy-to-use Facebook template that integrate your tech and creative talent
  • Leverage real-time social media campaigns
