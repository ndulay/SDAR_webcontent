Coronado Event for Chili Chefs and Proud Pet Owners

Plan on a hot time in Coronado on September 22, with pets and chili cooks competing at the Coronado Real Estate Association and City of Coronado's 2nd annual "Pet Parade & Chili Cook-Off" at Promenade Park on Strand Way. The event is from 11:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., and funds raised will benefit PAWS of Coronado. For more information, click here, or call (619) 435-5200.
