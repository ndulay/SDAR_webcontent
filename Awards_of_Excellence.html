Nominations are now being accepted for SDAR's annual Awards of Excellence, which acknowledge outstanding service to the Association, its members, the community, and the real estate industry. They reflect the ethics, integrity, and professional standards of the individuals. Finalists will be announced in December, with winners recognized at the Installation of Officers and Directors on January 11, 2014.

Who can submit nominations? Any SDAR member in good standing, including brokers, agents, managers, & affiliates. SELF-NOMINATIONS WILL BE ACCEPTED.

Note: Broker nominees must be active real estate brokers who supervise one or more agents. If not supervising agents, broker will qualify for REALTOR® category. Not eligible: 2013 Officers, members of Awards Selection Committee, Subcommittee, and category winners within the past three years.

Nominations must be received by FRIDAY, OCTOBER 25.
