2014 Mileage Rates

For 2014, business mileage rate is 56 cents per mile.

For 2014, miles driven for medical or moving purposes is 23.5 cents per mile.

For 2014, miles driven in service for a charitable organization is 14 cents per mile.

2013 Mileage Rates

For 2013, business mileage rate is 56.5 cents per mile.

For 2013, miles driven for moving purposes is 23 cents per mile.

For 2013, miles driven in service for a charitable organization is 14 cents per mile.

Information to Brokers

1099 Reporting for Real Estate Commissions Real estate brokers generally must file Form 1099-MISC for commission payments of $600 or more. For more information, visit the California Franchise Tax Board web site at www.ftb.ca.gov
