Are You Interested in a Career in Real Estate?

Real estate offers a wide range of career choices. You have the flexibility and freedom to set your own pace and there are no limits on your career achievement. As a real estate professional, you can choose to work in a large firm or run an independent office. Successful real estate professionals are goal-oriented, self-motivated, ambitious and people-oriented.

You can choose to specialize in many areas. Becoming a REALTOR® is among the most popular; a REALTOR® is a member of the National Association of REALTORS® and abides by its strict Code of Ethics.

Many buyers and sellers prefer working with REALTORS® because they are part of a trusted national organization with over 1 million members. In order to be a REALTOR® you must also join the California Associations of REALTORS® and a local association, such as the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS®. Membership benefits include access to industry events, accredited education, discounts on industry-related products, risk mitigation services, and much more.

Potential Careers in Real Estate include:

  • Residential Brokerage
  • Commercial Brokerage
  • Industrial and Office Brokerage
  • Farm and Land Brokerage
  • Real Estate Appraising
  • Property Management
  • Land Development
  • Urban Planning
  • Real Estate Counseling

Real estate is a rewarding career, offering flexibility, freedom and the opportunity to build a secure future for yourself while helping shape your community.
