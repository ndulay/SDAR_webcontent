Online Offer Submission - Directly to your MLS account

The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) and transaction management software provider eBrokerHouse, Inc. are pleased to announce a groundbreaking agreement, which makes the eBrokerHouse platform available to SDAR members!

The service is fully integrated with SDAR's MLS (SANDICOR®) and will enhance the ability of SDAR members to manage their businesses and serve their clients.

What is eBrokerHouse?

eBrokerHouse is an online offer submission platform that fully integrates with the Sandicor® MLS system, offering SDAR members the ability to manage their business and serve their clients in one, cloud based platform.
