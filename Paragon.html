Paragon is Here

Paragon replaces Tempo and Fusion and will work on both a Mac and PC with any browser. It also works on whatever mobile device you use to access the MLS, whether it's a smartphone or tablet. Paragon provides multi-tasking capabilities that allow agents to save time conducting property searches, creating comparative market analyses (CMAs), updating listing information, and completing other tasks in a single view on their computers.
