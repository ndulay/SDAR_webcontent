Applications for 2014 California Association of REALTORS® (C.A.R.) Directors from SDAR are now being taken. Deadline is April 1, 2013. Applicants must be a REALTOR® member of SDAR and currently active in the association.

A primary activity of C.A.R. is its legislative and political action program. Directors vote to set policy on legislative issues of vital importance to the real estate industry and private property rights. Directors are required to attend three business meetings a year. Before submitting your application, please make sure that your schedule permits you to attend the 2014 meetings on the following dates: January 23-26 in Monterey; May 1-4 in Sacramento; and October 9-12 in Long Beach.
