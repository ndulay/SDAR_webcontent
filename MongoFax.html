Close deals faster with this convenient San Diego Association of REALTORS® Member Benefit. MongoFAX empowers all San Diego REALTORS® to instantly fax paper-based documents (disclosure packages, signed contracts, etc.) directly to any email addresses in the world. All you need is an ordinary fax machine and SDAR’s designated MongoFAX Cover Page.

Instructions:

  1. SAVE Cover Page to Your Desktop
  2. TYPE Email Addresses
  3. PRINT Cover Page
  4. FAX Cover Page with Your Hardcopy Documents through the Phone Number Located at the bottom of the Cover Page

That’s it! Your documents will instantly be converted and routed to your desired recipient’s email address by MongoFAX’s secure, automated systems. No signups, registrations or passwords required.
