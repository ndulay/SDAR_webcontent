Light a Tree for Newtown, CT

Community invited to decorate 26 Christmas trees in remembrance of 20 children and 6 adult victims

When: Thursday, December 20, at 5:30 p.m.
Where: 4845 Ronson Court
San Diego, CA 92111
Map & Directions
Front lawn of the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) main offices in Kearny Mesa.

20 miniature Christmas trees and six grown ones will be up all day on Thursday. You are invited to stop during the day to decorate the trees, leave notes, letters, etc. SDAR will mail the notes and mementos to Newtown.


The tree lighting ceremony at 5:30 p.m. on Thursday will feature community speakers.

For more information, contact SDAR at (858) 715-8000.
