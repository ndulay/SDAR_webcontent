To customers of SDAR’s Del Mar Service Center,

The Flower Hill Promenade is being completely renovated, but your Del Mar Service Center is open for business. During construction at the retail center, SDAR’s Service Center, located at Suite D130, will continue to provide full membership services, a store for real estate products, and a classroom for education and professional development.

The renovation to Flower Hill includes updates such as new facades, stucco, new paved walkways and pavers, more parking, and landscaping. Completion is expected by mid-November. In the meantime, more parking spaces have been added along with defined access routes to the interior shops as well as temporary directory signage to assist customers.

Thank you for your patronage of the Del Mar Service Center.

Business hours are Monday, 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.; and Tuesday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. The address is 2690 Via De La Valle, Suite D130, Del Mar, CA 92014. For more information, call (858) 715-8061 or e-mail membership@sdar.com.
