Dear SDAR REALTOR®,
Our records indicate that you might not have completed your Mandatory NAR Ethics requirement.

There are several options available to satisfy this requirement. If you prefer live classes, currently there are two NAR Ethic courses scheduled at the Kearny Mesa location. You will also receive four hours of DRE credit that may be applied toward your license renewal.

  • Tuesday, July 24, 9:00 am - 1:00 pm
  • Monday, Sept. 17, 8:00 am - 12:00 pm (during Fall Extravaganza)


You can also take the Online Ethics Course at Realtor.org, but you will not receive DRE credit. However, the course is FREE. Once you have completed the course, NAR will e-mail you a certificate which you can forward to membership@sdar.com and Member Services will update your records.

Additional online options: Take the course online at the CE Shop. Be sure to write down the promo code ‘PROMO20’ to receive a 20% discount off the price or take the C.A.R. online series and receive a 15% discount.

PLEASE NOTE: All educational providers that provide Ethics courses may not meet NAR's requirement. The training must meet specific learning objectives and criteria established by the National Association of REALTORS®, and although they satisfy the DRE for license renewal, they may not satisfy the Mandated NAR Ethics requirement.

If you feel you have received this e-mail in error and you have satisfied your NAR Ethics requirement, please contact Member Services.

Thank You.
