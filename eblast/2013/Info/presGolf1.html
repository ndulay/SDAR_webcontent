Watch Video 		I’m personally inviting you to join your colleagues and invite your friends to the Annual Everyday Heroes Golf Tournament on Friday, August 10, at the Carmel Mountain Ranch Country Club.

SDAR hosts this fundraiser each year to benefit the Ambassadors Foundation, a 501(c)3 non-profit charity, which helps San Diego police officers become homeowners through financial assistance and education.

Even if you can’t make the tournament (or if you don’t golf), please plan to attend the evening reception, dinner, auctions and awards ceremony at the clubhouse. Dinner tickets are only $50.

Let’s have a good showing of our SDAR Leadership. I’d love to see you there!
