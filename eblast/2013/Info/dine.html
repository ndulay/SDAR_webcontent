Dine & Dialogue with San Diego County Treasurer


SDAR Government Affairs Chair Ed Smith would like you to join him at the East County Chamber of Commerce which is hosting San Diego County Treasurer Dan McAllister for an informative "Dine & Dialogue" on June 25. McAllister will speak about how our county is dealing with a challenging economy and how it affects you and your business. He will also address the impact of property devaluation on the county.

Date: Monday, June 25
Time: 11:45 a.m. to 1:15 p.m.
Location: San Diego East County Chamber of Commerce
Chamber Board Room
201 S. Magnolia Ave, El Cajon, CA 92020
Map & Directions
Lunch: Provided
RSVP: By Friday, June 22 to Jarryd Davis, Vice President of Marketing and Events for the San Diego East County Chamber of Commerce. (Note: Due to size of the Board Room, only the first 30 RSVPs can be accepted.)
