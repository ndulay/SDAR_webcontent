Realist Announcement of Telephone Number Suppression

Sandicor wants to make you aware of an important change to some of the information delivered by Realist®, the tax database provided as part of your membership with Sandicor.

Currently, some properties in the tax database include the property owner’s phone number.   On Tuesday, December 11, 2012, these phone numbers will be no longer be displayed. Please be advised this change will impact all Realist® features pertaining to telephone numbers, including display, search, and export options.

The decision to make this change was predicated on compliance with all applicable state and federal regulations in connection with telemarketing activities, including the FTC’s DNC guidelines.


Please be sure to mark messages@mysdmls.com as a safe email address so that you continue to receive important updates to your MLS account.
