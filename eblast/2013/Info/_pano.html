Coming Soon: Property Panorama’s Complete Suite of Multi Media Tours

SDAR, Sandicor and Property Panorama have come together to offer a range of virtual tour products, at little or no cost, for all MLS Subscribers beginning May 15, 2012.

As part of your MLS Subscriber benefits, you will receive InstaView Virtual Tours and InstaCast Video Tours FREE OF CHARGE for all your listings. To enhance your listings using higher functions and features, Sandicor has negotiated deeply discounted pricing for Property Panorama’s InstaViewPRO, InstaPrint and Elite Edition products. In addition to low pricing, Property Panorama offers “one for all licensing”, which means when you license a product for one of your listings, you license that same product for ALL of your listings.

The following InstaTour Products were designed to minimize your efforts in creating professional quality virtual tour products:

  • InstaView Virtual Tours - FREE - Each time a new listing is entered in TEMPO/FUSION, Property Panorama automatically merges the listing data and property images (the first 5 images are free) to create a professional slide show tour which is attached to your listing. You do nothing! When you make changes to your listing, InstaView is automatically updated.
  • InstaCast Video Tour - FREE - Property Panorama takes the InstaView data and automatically creates a professional video tour which includes music and professional transitions from scene to scene. The Video Tour is automatically uploaded to YouTube and is also updated whenever you make changes to your listing in TEMPO/FUSION. Again, you do nothing!
  • InstaView Pro Virtual Tours (available June 15, 2012) - This highly customizable, branded virtual tour has many additional options and features including: a customizable layout, choices of theme and music, agent/brokerage branding options, unlimited images, full voiceover features, integrated maps, and school information. The cost “for all your listings” is $8.49/month or $84.99/annually prepaid.
  • InstaPrint Virtual Flyers (available June 15, 2012) - This BRAND NEW service automatically creates a totally customizable online Virtual Flyer and high-definition printable PDF for each listing! InstaPrint is great for lead generation. If a consumer requests your online flyer, Property Panorama passes the contact information to you! The cost “for all your listings” is $3.99/month or $39.99/annually prepaid.
  • Elite Suite (available May 15, 2012) - Property Panorama’s Elite Suite is their flagship product. The Elite Suite is a bundled collection of 5 different, advanced feature and function virtual presentations which enable you to create and distribute fully-customizable virtual tours, quickly and easily. The cost for all five virtual tour services “for all your listings” is $12.49/month or $124.99/annually prepaid. The 5 features include:
    1. Panoramic Virtual Tours - Using your property images in TEMPO/FUSION, Property Panorama delivers a panoramic virtual tour and automatically attaches an unbranded version to TEMPO/FUSION and a fully branded version to Zillow and Trulia. You can use the same link for you and your Broker’s websites or post the link on Facebook and other social networking sites.
    2. MobileMedia Tours with TurboText - A sign on your listing instructs a person to “Text 984323 to 440-725-TOUR to see a full video tour of this home”! When a prospect texts in the code, they will receive a reply text seconds later that includes your contact information, price, square footage, beds, baths, other basic information, as well as a link to the video tour of the listing that is specially formatted to work on any cell phone in the world. Competitive TurboText products sell for $20-25 per month and are included FREE with Property Panorama’s Elite Suite.
    3. Premium Video Tours- A premium level video tour is created by Property Panorama using custom-created graphics, professional transitions, exclusive music, key words and other special effects.
    4. PodCAST Tours - Automatically created each time an Elite Edition is created, with no additional effort on your part. After downloaded to your iPod, Zune, PSP or similar device, you can show multiple homes with similar characteristics to buyers, thus eliminating their need to visit numerous properties.
    5. CD/DVD Tours - To showcase you, your listings and your brokerage, CD/DVD Tours can be handed out to visitors at open houses.

All of Property Panorama products include online tutorials, online chat support, and a toll-free number which connects you to a professional Customer Specialist.
