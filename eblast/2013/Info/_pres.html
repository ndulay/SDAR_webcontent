Dear SDAR Member,

As your president for 2012, I am happy to report SDAR is constantly improving services, increasing benefits, ensuring value, and strengthening the association so we are in the best position to meet challenges that lie ahead. This is a quick mid-year report on a few of the many initiatives to better serve you and to make sure you have the tools, resources, and information to be more successful than ever.

Membership: Our membership has remained healthy at 10,500-plus, while most associations have experienced an approximately 18% decrease. Each month SDAR welcomes about 75 new members.

Financial: Considine & Considine, an independent auditor, recently completed our annual audit which was reported to the board that SDAR is financially healthy.

Click here to view the Auditor’s Report.
Click here to view the Condensed Financial Statements.

Education: We have held 167 classes with 3,770 attending students. Click here for a list of upcoming classes.

Service Centers: This year we have serviced more than 2,967 members at our main office, and 5,555 members have been serviced in our South Bay, East County, Coronado, Ranch Bernardo, and Del Mar service centers. Click here for a list of our service center locations and business hours.

Events: Since January SDAR has hosted 19 events serving more than 3,100 members. Click here for a list of upcoming events.

On May 30 we presented the annual REALTOR® Expo and a Mayoral Debate serving nearly 2,000 attendees. REALTORS® attending the Expo learned about the tools and resources available to help their business like Google Apps, REALTORS® Property Resource, and more. Click here for the Expo Online.

New Initiatives Launched:

  • Broker Exchanges for SDAR and brokers to learn what brokers are doing and what they need
  • InFocus newsletter - real estate statistics in partnership with The London Group
  • Presidential Advisory Group roundtables to discuss the state of the economy with local brokers
  • Going Google to offer many exciting cost saving technology products

New Initiatives Planned and Coming:

  • SDAR Website redesign to provide members and consumers with a one-stop experience to find anything related real estate
  • REALTOR®DASH - a dashboard for SDAR members to access all the key platforms to better serve their clients

Several new products and services are already being planned for 2013. SDAR members will receive a survey to assess needs to ensure delivery of quality benefits at the best value. I encourage each of you to take the time to participate in the survey.

As you can see, we have already accomplished a lot for 2012. I am confident we will continue to provide you with the very best products, benefits, and services our industry offers. Thank you for being a member of SDAR.

Sincerely,


Donna Sanfilippo
2012 President
