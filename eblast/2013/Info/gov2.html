Dear SDAR Member,

To increase REALTOR® participation and influence in the political process, the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® has launched a voter registration drive among our members. 

While nearly 80 percent of all REALTORS® are registered to vote, we can do better. According to our information, we could not confirm that you are registered to vote.While you may be registered, we would like you to consider taking one of the following actions: 

If you know that you ARE NOT REGISTERED, and would like to REGISTER TO VOTE, it has never been easier.

Register to Vote Online
http://registertovote.ca.gov/

Register to vote at SDAR
Six conveniently located service centers. Pick up a voter registration form there.

Check out your REALTOR® Party Voter Guide

Monday October 22 is the last day register to vote.

 


If you ARE REGISTERED to vote, congratulations! We would appreciate your help in keeping our files up to date by going to www.RealtorActionCenter.com/vote and confirming your registration. Often we have only a business address on file, which explains why we could not match you to the state voter file.

The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® is proud to join with the National Association of REALTORS® on this important civic effort to increase the number of registered voters and voter participation across the country. Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact our Government Affairs Director Jordan Marks or Political Coordinator Kimberlee Theis at 858-715-8005 or via e-mail at govaffairs@SDAR.com .
