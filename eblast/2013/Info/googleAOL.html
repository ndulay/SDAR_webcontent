What Works for Your Professional Needs - AOL vs. Google?

As an AOL user, you already know the benefits it may have on your business, but have you considered the advantages of having a Google account?

The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) entered into an agreement with Google to introduce their technology and professional tools to the REALTOR® community. This strategic move is the first-of-its-kind, and you are a critical part of this historic change.

Why change? Because your business depends on it.

With Google Apps, you get more than e-mail!

  • Brand impact – Google is a clear winner in professional credibility, use your REALTOR® branded email provided by SDAR to strengthen your professional brand.
  • Accessibility – Access all your documents from any device with a web browser.
  • Storage - Virtually unlimited e-mail storage, up to 25 GB.
  • Integration of IM and e-mail - Send instant messages to your contacts that are online quickly.
  • Size of attachments – Stop worrying about bounce backs when sharing files. Google Apps allows for a larger attachment size, up to 10 GB.
  • Flexibility with attachments – Easily put up as many attachments as needed and send a large amount of attachments.
  • Shared documents - Create, edit, and share your documents, calendars, spreadsheets, and web pages online


We encourage you to activate your Google Apps account today to take advantage of this member benefit. Visit www.sdar.com/google or call (858) 715-8040.
