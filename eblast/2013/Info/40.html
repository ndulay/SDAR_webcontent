SUPPORT SAN DIEGO FAMILIES AFFECTED BY AUTISM
Birthday Celebration Raises Money for Autism Tree Project


Join CRASD in celebrating the 40th birthday of CRASD Board Member Curtis Gabhart of Gabhart Investments, and help raise $40,000 for the Autism Tree Project Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting San Diego families impacted by autism.

The May 23 fundraiser is at the Surfrider at the Del Mar Beach Colony, where you will enjoy food, drinks, live music, silent auction, raffles and door prizes. Tickets are $25 per person or $40 per couple, and all proceeds go to the foundation and are tax deductible.
