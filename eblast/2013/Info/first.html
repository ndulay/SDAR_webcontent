Expert Panel Assists First-Time Home Buyers This Saturday in El Cajon
Presented in partnership with Sen. Joel Anderson

Hosted by SDAR's Housing Opportunities Committee, a panel of experts will cover topics such as choosing a REALTOR®, down payment assistance, special loans and grants, and tax credits. You are encouraged to bring your clients and inform your contacts about this free event taking place this Saturday at SDAR’s East County Service Center.
Date: Saturday, July 14
Time: 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
Location: SDAR East County Service Center
220 West Main Street, El Cajon, CA 92020
Map & Directions
Cost: Free (please register, but walk-ins welcomed)
Register: Contact Kimberlee Theis or call (858) 715-8005.
