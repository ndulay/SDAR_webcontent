Auction Items Needed for 5th Annual Everyday Heroes Golf Tournament

Dear SDAR Board of Directors and Committee Members,

Just a friendly reminder that we are approaching our 5th Annual Everyday Heroes Golf Tournament on August 10, 2012. This fun filled event benefits the Ambassadors Foundation a 501(c)3 non-profit charity that assists San Diego police officers in realizing the dream of homeownership. All of the proceeds from our auction will help a San Diego police officer and his/her family.

In order to make this year’s Live and Silent Auction a resounding success we are seeking help with the items listed below:

  • Gift certificates for:
    • Hotels
    • Travel
    • Spa packages
    • Shopping
    • Restaurants
  • Unique one-of-a-kind experiences such as:
    • Dine with a celebrity
    • Announce a sports game
    • Walk-on TV appearances
    • Drive a NASCAR
    • Tickets to the Holiday Bowl, Pac 12 Football Championship, or Rose Parade
    • Tickets to Fashion Week in New York
    • Tickets to Farmers Insurance Open
  • Other items:
    • Ski passes
    • Wine
    • Jewelry
    • Golf Clubs
    • Charger tickets
    • Private catered dinner for 8 at your home


The service or products donated will gain valuable exposure while also being tax deductible. In addition to auction items, monetary donations are also encouraged. All donations are tax deductible, tax ID# 20-8769322.

We hope that you will support a worthy cause and thank you for your consideration to support our police officers. Your donation is greatly appreciated.

For more information and to RSVP for the golf tournament or dinner please visit www.sdar.com/golf or call 858-715-8035. Monetary donations are also accepted.

Sincerely,


Donna Sanfilippo
2012 SDAR President
