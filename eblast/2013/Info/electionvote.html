SDAR’s Annual Election of 2013 Officers and Directors concludes Thursday, August 30, at 5:00 p.m. Eligible voters already should have received an electronic ballot and PIN information by e-mail. If you have not voted or don’t have your original e-mail, you will receive a reminder on Wednesday from the e-mail address “SDARElection,” from which you will be able to vote directly, or you may vote when you visit the SDAR website or one of our SDAR Service Centers (as long as you have your PIN information).
