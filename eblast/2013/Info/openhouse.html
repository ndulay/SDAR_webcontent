Nationwide Open House Weekend – April 28 and 29


Dear SDAR Member:

SDAR is pleased to be participating in the REALTOR® Nationwide Open House Weekend April 28 and 29. This event provides residents throughout the country with a chance to view homes in specific neighborhoods as the home buying season officially kicks off. We hope you will participate by holding an open house on that weekend and displaying balloons so potential home buyers take notice.


Here’s how you can participate:

  • Stop by one of SDAR’s Service Centers and pick up your FREE balloons (while supplies last)
  • Post your open houses for FREE on SDAR’s website. To submit your open houses, click here.


For a convenient “Field Guide” for conducting open houses, click here. We hope you will participate in the REALTOR® Open House Weekend April 28 and 29!
