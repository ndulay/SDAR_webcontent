Press Conference on Wednesday, June 20, at 11:00 a.m.

Dear SDAR Member,

SDAR will host a press conference this Wednesday, June 20, to announce the association’s endorsement of Carl DeMaio for San Diego Mayor. Please attend the event so that we can show our support for Carl!

Carl has expressed strong interest in helping us with several of our issues like Vacant Property, Business License Tax, and Lead Based Paint, to name a few. In meeting with SDAR’s Executive Committee, Carl was extremely open about his vision for the City of San Diego, and was very interested in seeking our support as well as REALTOR® involvement for his campaign and as our next mayor.

Date: Wednesday, June 20
Time: 11:00 a.m.
Location: SDAR Kearny Mesa Service Center
4845 Ronson Court, San Diego, CA 92111

We hope to see you there!
