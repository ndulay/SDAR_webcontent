SDAR is pleased to announce that access to REALTORS® Property Resource™ (RPR™) became available on Monday, June 11 via a SmartLink in the My Sidebar Options/Resources of TEMPO™ and Fusion™ as well as a link on the Details page of a listing! Please note, you will need your NRDS ID number to register. If you cannot remember your NRDS ID number, please contact your service center.


RPR™ is a national database of robust, property centric information covering every parcel of property in the country! This database is designed to be a resource for NAR® members, creating a single source access to data which will add value to the information available for REALTORS® to use with their clients and customers. Property centric information will be linked to MLS listings and includes tax assessment, zoning, school districts, demographics, public record information, notice of default, REO, foreclosure data, and so much more! Included in the RPR™ services is a REALTORS® Valuation Model (RVM) which offers several different options, such as reports of a home’s estimated value, historic property comparisons, a broker/manager resource center, downloadable marketing pieces, live and recorded training sessions, and a user-friendly mobile App.

It is the goal of RPR™ to reinforce a REALTOR’s® value in today’s market place by keeping them in the forefront of today’s dynamic technology curve thereby better able to serve their skilled, savvy clients. Currently, RPR™ has more than 500,000 REALTOR® members from more than 270 MLSs nationwide. As part of this agreement, RPR provides risk analytic tools for the mortgage industry such as the RVM. The risk analytic products are used by lenders internally and never provided to or shared with members of the public.

Brokers who do not wish to allow their listings be used as part of the RPR™ analytics services may choose to opt out their listings from RPR™ by clicking on the following link Real-Out@sandicor.com. In your response, please include your name and Broker MLS number. Please note, by opting out of RPR™, your listings are opted out of the entire RPR™ system and will not be viewed or used by you or your agents in the RPR™ system. RPR provides a valuable assortment of productive tools and services.
