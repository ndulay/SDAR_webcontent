Light A Tree for Newtown, CT

Community invited to decorate 26 Christmas trees in remembrance of 20 children and 6 adult victims

When: Now through Christmas
Where: 4845 Ronson Court
San Diego, CA 92111
Map & Directions
Front lawn of the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) main offices in Kearny Mesa.

20 miniature Christmas trees and six grown ones are displayed. You are invited to stop during the day to decorate the trees, leave notes, letters, etc. SDAR will mail the notes and mementos to Newtown.
