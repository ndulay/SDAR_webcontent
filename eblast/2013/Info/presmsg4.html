Dear Members:
Please join me in welcoming Bob Filner as San Diego’s new mayor. We look forward to working with Mayor Filner to continue the tremendous recovery we have seen in San Diego’s real estate market and create new paths to homeownership.

Join me this Monday, December 3, for Mayor Filner’s swearing-in at 10:00 a.m. at the Balboa Park Club in Balboa Park. In addition, that evening there will be multiple community celebrations throughout the City of San Diego, which has been listed below for your convenience. Please stop by an event and welcome our new mayor as community member and as a member of the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS®.

Sincerely,

Donna Sanfilippo
SDAR 2012 President

www.sdar.com

P.S. If you are unable to meet Mayor Filner on his inauguration day, then join him at our Holiday House Celebration on December 6.
