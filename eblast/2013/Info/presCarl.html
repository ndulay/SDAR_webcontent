Dear SDAR Member,

This week the San Diego Association of REALTORS® announced the endorsement of Councilmember Carl DeMaio for Mayor of San Diego. The leadership of this association spent time with all the mayoral candidates to make certain we are supporting the right leader who can build on the financial reform our city has desperately needed, and we looked for a leader that will move us forward to even greater levels of success in the coming years.

Carl DeMaio’s experience and leadership on pension reform and fighting tax increases on small businesses make him the only candidate with the track record needed to bring more jobs, better roads, stronger communities, and safer neighborhoods to San Diego. As REALTORS®, we are committed to protecting San Diego and the American Dream of homeownership, and we know Carl DeMaio is committed to that, too.
