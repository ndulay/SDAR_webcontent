Notice of Special Election Regarding Future Elections of Officers and Board Members

(NOTE AMENDED DATES OF ELECTION)


A Special Election of the membership of the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) will take place April 10 to 19 to decide whether the association will adopt a new organizational model by which:

    1. The full membership will elect the Board of Directors, and
    2. The elected Board of Directors will appoint the Officers

Background: The SDAR Nominating and Election Task Force studied the 15 largest REALTOR® Associations in the country, as well as several non-REALTOR® organizations. One area that enabled these other associations to provide a consistent approach to delivering the needed short- and long-term benefits to their members was their volunteer representation on the board selection of the officers. Specifically, the Task Force learned that all the larger associations with complex business and management models were more efficient and delivered more benefits to the members with an organizational structure set up for the full membership to elect the Board of Directors and for the Board to appoint the officers of the association (for SDAR, that would be the President-Elect, Treasurer, and Vice President). After studying, discussing and much careful consideration, the Task Force, Executive Committee and Board of Directors, comprising a total of over 40 volunteers, recommends that SDAR’s membership adopt a similar organizational model.

How Voting Will Take Place:
Eligible voters will receive an electronic ballot and PIN information by e-mail at the start of voting at 8:00 a.m. on Wednesday, April 10.

(To be eligible to vote, you must be an active REALTOR® member. New members must have attended New Member Orientation at least by February 4, 2013.)


You will be able to vote directly from the e-mail ballot, or you may vote when you visit the SDAR website or one of our SDAR Service Centers (as long as you have your PIN information from the e-mail). The polls will remain open until 5:00 p.m. on Friday, April 19, and results will be posted on April 22 on SDAR’s website and in the Service Centers.

SDAR encourages all members to take part in this Special Election. Your participation is appreciated. If you have any questions, contact Betty or call (858) 715-8006.
