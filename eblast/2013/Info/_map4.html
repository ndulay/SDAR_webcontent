WHAT IS MAP?

  • MAP is the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS®’s (SDAR) new initiative that will provide our members a direct line of communication to have their voice heard.
  • Your insights will help us to fine tune current offerings and provide a roadmap for future products and services.
  • You will serve as our industry’s “eyes and ears”


WHAT TO EXPECT:

  • You will be invited to participate in surveys and other beta programs
  • Survey highlights will be posted on www.SDAR.com.


WHAT IS NEXT?

 To get started, we invite you to become a member of MAP by completing the questionnaire below.


 

WE WANT YOUR VOICE TO BE HEARD! Help us to continue to shape and map out the future of what already has become one of the best trade associations in the county.

Thank you,
SDAR’s MAP team
