Letter from YPN Vice Chair,

The market is heating up and it is time to be part of the immense opportunities ahead of us.

Please join us on May 30th for an informative luncheon at the San Diego County 2012 REALTOR® Expo and Conference where San Diego’s mayoral candidates will be posed questions by a media panel on a variety of business issues affecting real estate. The Mayoral Debate luncheon will be held at the Town & Country Resort and Convention Center from 11:45 a.m. – 1:15 p.m.

We are in one of the most interesting times ever, as a market place, political landscape and community. It is a critical time for such a young and diverse group of professionals such as SDAR’s YPN to have a voice for change and to make a difference. With knowledge comes power and with some understanding of the political process we have the ability to be properly informed on many of the current issues.

No matter your political affiliation you should take the time to understand how politics affect your everyday lives. Start to become a person who is committed to your success as well as pay respect for those who came before to lay a ground work of opportunity. Join us on May 30th for the last major mayoral debate at the San Diego County 2012 REALTOR® Expo and Conference.

For more information or to register please visit lastmmdebate.eventbrite.com and be sure to request to sit at the YPN designated table(s).
