Holiday House Celebration at Driftwood in Olde Del Mar - Join Us on Dec. 6

The Holiday House is the San Diego real estate community’s initiative to help local:

  • children
  • military
  • homeless
  • senior citizens

We’ll be filling this luxurious home with:

  • new toys
  • new clothes
  • new household items
  • non-perishable food


Last year’s drive delivered over $10,000 in donations and gifts to local charities thanks to the community’s generous outpouring of support.

Date: Thursday, December 6
Time: 4:00 p.m. – 8:00 p.m.
Note Earlier Start Time!
Location: The Driftwood in Olde Del Mar
1930 Seaview Ave
Del Mar, CA 92014

Cost: Please bring a donation of items or gift cards valued at a minimum of $25, or make a financial contribution of $25 or more.
