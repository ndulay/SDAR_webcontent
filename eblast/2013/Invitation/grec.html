Fast Facts: Why should you be interested in assisting foreign real estate clients?
According to recent national surveys:

  • 27% of REALTORS® had some international clientele in the past year
  • 52% of REALTORS® reported that international transactions accounted between 1 to 10% of their total transactions
  • 51% of all purchasing activity in the U.S. by international clients is focused on four states (California, Florida, Texas, and Arizona)
  • California is a top destination for foreign investors (12% of all home purchases, with most coming from Mexico, the Philippines, China, India, and Vietnam)
