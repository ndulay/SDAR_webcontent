Gmail Users: Go to the Next Level with the Power of Google

As a Gmail account holder, you know that Google’s e-mail can be more intuitive, efficient and useful. SDAR is now prepared to take you to the next level, since we are going Google.

Going Google
With Google Apps, your important information – e-mail, documents, and websites – now live in a “cloud” so they can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection, at a property, from the office, or from home. This time savings should translate into more satisfied clients and more business for you.

With Google Sites, you’ll able to create websites for buyers with new listings and open houses, information on neighborhoods and schools, as well as directions and maps to each property. You can also create sites for properties on the market. Instead of calling or e-mailing clients, you can just update your site, so buyers and sellers always have one place to go.
