Exclusive Opportunity to Register Early for Golf Tournament on August 10

Break out your golf clubs to compete in the 5th Annual Everyday Heroes Golf Tournament. Proceeds benefit the Ambassadors Foundation, a 501(c)3 non-profit charity, that assists San Diego police officers to become homeowners through financial assistance and education.

Early-bird pricing ends July 13!

Police officers put it all on the line to protect our communities every day, yet San Diego currently has the lowest ratios of police officers to homeowners. Together we can help make San Diego a better place to live while helping make the dream of homeownership for these heroes a reality!

Join us for dinner, which includes the silent auction, live auction, and awards ceremony. Golfers and non-golfers are welcome for dinner.
