Candidate Training Academy - Oct. 26

Be the Next Voice of the REALTOR® Community
Join SDAR for a day of training with National Association of REALTORS® campaign experts and learn how to market yourself to the public and run a political campaign. The academy covers topics such as why someone should run for office, campaign planning and budgeting, research and targeting, fundraising, voter contact, winning online, and getting out the vote. SDAR is one of only 20 associations chosen by NAR to host this event!
