Awards of Excellence: Nominate a Broker or Agent Today!

Nominations are now being accepted for SDAR's annual Awards of Excellence, which acknowledge outstanding service to the Association, its members, the community, and the real estate industry. They reflect the ethics, integrity, and professional standards of the individuals.

Nominations are in four categories: Broker, Agent, Office Manager, and Affiliate. Self nominations are accepted. The 2014 Awards of Excellence recipients will be presented at the annual Installation Dinner held January 2015.

BRE Newsletter Includes In-Depth Articles on Violations

The recent California Bureau of Real Estate newsletter includes enlightening articles about its protocols for brokers to report violations (the new "Benefit of the Doubt" program).

The CalBRE also recently finalized its authority to issue citations and assess administrative fines to violators of the California Real Estate Law.
