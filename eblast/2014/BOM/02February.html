Service Animals and No-Pet Policies

At a recent NAR risk management meeting, some members reported experiencing a growing trend of requests being made to landlords and condo associations for exceptions to a no-pet policy for accommodating a tenant or owner with a physical or mental disability. NAR has produced an informative 5-minute video on this topic to help real estate professionals understand the law regarding these requests.

Advice on Setting Up Real Estate Broker Trust Accounts

Any real estate broker who accepts funds belonging to others is required to deposit those funds into one of three places within three business days following receipt, one of which is a trust fund account. The California Bureau of Real Estate (CalBRE) offers some background and advice in establishing a Broker Trust Account.
