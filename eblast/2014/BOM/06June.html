Hackers Intercept Emails to Steal Wired Funds

Warnings have been issued about the rise of a fraud scam being used to steal wired funds in real estate transactions. After hacking into the email accounts of parties in a real estate transaction, hackers then send legitimate-looking emails purportedly from the escrow or title company directing the buyer to wire transfer funds which end of going to the hacker's account. Buyers and sellers should be advised to confirm all emailed wiring instructions directly with the escrow or title company. Learn more about internet crime schemes and how to report them.
