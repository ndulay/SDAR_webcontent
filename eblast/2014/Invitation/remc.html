Attorney & REALTOR® Networking Mixer!


The San Diego County Bar Association and the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® invite you to attend an Attorney and REALTOR® Networking Mixer on November 15, sponsored by the Real Estate Mediation Center.

Make important business contacts with real estate attorneys, fellow REALTORS® and mediators, while enjoying appetizers and refreshments. 

This is not a sit-down, buttoned-up event. Shake hands, make new contacts, discuss hot topics and build your network. 

The mixer will provide a unique opportunity for you to connect and communicate with potential clients in the legal community. Join real estate professionals sharing the common goals of creating relationships, mitigating risk and discussing future opportunities. 

Benefits of attending:

  • Building relationships
  • Networking
  • Generating leads
  • Discussing real estate issues
  • Connecting with the leaders in real estate

The event is free for invited guests. Please register below. 


Date: Thursday, November 15
Time: 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.
Location:

Randy Jones All American Sports Grill
7510 Hazard Center Drive
San Diego, CA 92108
Map & Directions
Cost: Free (includes appetizers and 1 drink ticket)
