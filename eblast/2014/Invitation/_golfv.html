Watch Video		 Break out your golf clubs and compete in the Annual Everyday Heroes Golf Tournament on Friday, August 10. Proceeds benefit the Ambassadors Foundation, a 501(c)3 non-profit charity, which helps San Diego police officers become homeowners through financial assistance and education.
