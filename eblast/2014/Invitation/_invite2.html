Elevate Your Status as the Host of the Holiday House

The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) is pleased to invite you to be the Holiday House Premier Host Facility for 2012! Your vacant listing could be the site of the Holiday House Celebration on December 6 and a venue for collection of new toys, clothing, and household goods for San Diego charities during our Holiday House initiative.

Holiday House is the San Diego real estate community’s effort to help local children, military, homeless, and senior citizens. Donation sites are located throughout the community, with the closing celebration held on December 6 at the Holiday House facility.
