Volunteer to Make a Difference – Saturday, April 28

Lunch provided
SDAR is pleased to be supporting Rebuilding Together, San Diego, again this year for their annual Rebuilding Day scheduled for SATURDAY, APRIL 28. We hope you will join us for this worthwhile project.

This year our team will help rehab the home of an elderly couple in Santee who are financially and physically unable to care for their residence. Our efforts will help ensure they will have a safe and habitable home moving forward. We need volunteers to help with a large amount of landscaping, including weed abatement and tree/bush removal. The home has an overgrown yard lending concern to a potential fire hazard. We will also be painting the exterior of the house and making other minor general repairs to the home.

All skill levels are needed and welcomed. Volunteers are welcome to work all day, or on the morning or afternoon shifts. We appreciate help in the afternoon to make sure we finish all projects. As always, we are working with limited funding. If you are unable to volunteer on April 28, you can still provide much needed assistance through in-kind donations. Monetary donations are also welcomed. Both are tax deductible.

Many hands make light work! To register, contact Events@sdar.com or download the Fax-Back Registration Form, or call (858) 715-8031; children under the age of 16 are not able to volunteer due to federal law. To learn more, or to contribute in-kind or monetary donations, please click here and click on the Donate Now web page. Feel free to distribute the flyer to colleagues and friends. More information will be sent to registrants closer to the day of the event. We look forward to you joining us for what is sure to be another rewarding experience.
