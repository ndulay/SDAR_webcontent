2012 Luncheon Features Mayoral Debate
Don’t miss an exciting opportunity to attend the Mayoral Debate moderated by George Chamberlin, Executive Editor of The Daily Transcript. Join your colleagues for the last Mayoral Debate before the election. Local media will ask San Diego’s mayoral candidates Carl DeMaio, Bonnie Dumanis, Bob Filner, and Nathan Fletcher challenging questions on legislative issues impacting San Diego’s real estate industry. Get to know the candidates in an intimate setting before you place your vote on election day.
