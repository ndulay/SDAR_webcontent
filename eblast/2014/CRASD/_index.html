Commercial Alliance Dealmaker Awards & Economic Summit


Don’t miss the Commercial Real Estate Alliance of San Diego’s 2012 Dealmaker Awards and Economic Summit on February 9. Keynote speaker is Gary H. London, president of The London Group Realty Advisors, who will offer an insightful discussion about commercial real estate issues impacting Southern California. The breakfast event includes the Installation of 2012 CRASD Officers and Directors, the annual Dealmaker Awards, and the guest emcee is George Chamberlin, Executive Editor of The Daily Transcript.

Date:

Thursday, February 9, 2012

Time:

7:30 a.m. – 10:30 a.m.

Location:

Handlery Hotel & Resort
950 Hotel Circle North, San Diego, 92108
Map and Directions

Cost:

$29 for CRASD Members
$39 for Non-Members
(Advance registration required. Price includes continental breakfast.)
