Member Appreciation Week is Oct. 13-17
Lunch and Events All Week at 6 Service Centers

SDAR couldn't fit its celebration into a single day, so Member Appreciation Week will include lunch EACH DAY from 12:00 to 1:00 p.m. at our six service centers throughout the county. Then, on specific days of the week, we're throwing a Member Party at one of the service centers near you from 11:30 a.m to 4:30 p.m., including prizes and giveaways, product demonstrations, massages, and fitness consultations.
