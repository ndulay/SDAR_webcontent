New Laws & CalBRE Updates You Need to Know

Must-Attend Seminar on Jan. 28 at Town & Country
Everything you need to know about 2014 new laws affecting real estate, plus updates from the California Bureau of Real Estate (CalBRE), will be presented on Tuesday, January 28, at SDAR's popular annual seminar. Officials from C.A.R., NAR, and the CalBRE will give the tools to navigate the rules and ensure your business success. A hot breakfast is included in the class fee, which supports SDAR's political advocacy efforts.
