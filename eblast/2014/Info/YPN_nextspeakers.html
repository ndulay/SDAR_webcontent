James Brennan,
Co-founder - 3/73 Management & Enlightened Hospitality Group
In 2002, James co-founded 3/73 Management and opened the Gas Lamp Quarter's: Side Bar, Ciro's Pizzeria, Stingaree, the Witherby, Bar West and Universal. In 2009, Brennan co-founded Enlightened Hospitality Group, which holds Stingaree, Searsucker, Herringbone, Gabardine, Campine Catering and Suja Juice. Brennan is referred to as San Diego's King of Nightlife and was named to the National Nightclub Top 100 List.

Matt Greene, President/ Founder - 6 Degrees
6 Degrees is a successful business networking group with over 650 members and 18,000 attendees. Matt is an expert in strategic planning, sales, member development and forming event and media partnerships. Matt was named San Diego Magazine's 2013 Mover to Watch, and received The San Diego Daily Transcript's Top Young Influential Award in 2012

Tom Del Monte, President/CTO - Interra Energy
Interra Energy is a biomass-to-biochar renewable energy company seeking to create sustainable agricultural practices and reduce greenhouse gasses. In 2012 Interra Energy's Biochar Soil was voted San Diego's "Most Eco Friendly Product" by Entrepreneur Day. In 2011, Interra won the Sustainability & Clean Tech Category in UCSD's Entrepreneur Challenge.

Kacee Johnson, Executive Vice President - Cloud9 Real Time
Cloud9 Real Time is a leader in secure cloud-based solutions. Overseeing its marketing, sales, and business development since 2009, Kacee has led its growth to over 16,000 users and increased its customer base by over 600%. She was recognized as one of 2012's "Top 40 Under 40" Executives by CPA Practice Advisor.

Chris Placencia, Founder - Jason Paul Marketing
Jason Paul Marketing helps businesses succeed through web development and strategic marketing. Chris is also the co-founder of 400 Media Group, The Dapper Press and 805 Nightlife. In 2013, he was voted "Top 40 Under 40" by San Diego Metro Magazine.
