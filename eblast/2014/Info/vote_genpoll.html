JUNE 3 ELECTION - REALTOR® VOTER GUIDE
REALTOR® PARTY HELPS PROTECT YOUR BOTTOM LINE


Many issues facing the REALTOR® community are voted on by our elected officials. The REALTOR® Party defends your bottom line by stopping point-of-sale mandates, guarding your commissions from additional taxation, and protecting the Mortgage Interest Deduction. We can only do so with the support of REALTOR® "champions."

Vote for the REALTOR® Party champions vetted and recommended by your local REALTOR® Political Affairs Committee, California Association of REALTORS®, and National Association of REALTORS® leaders.

To volunteer with any of the supported candidates please contact (858) 715-8026.
