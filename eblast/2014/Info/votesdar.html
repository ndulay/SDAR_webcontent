REMINDER TO VOTE FOR 2015 SDAR DIRECTORS
ELIGIBLE VOTERS CAN CAST BALLOTS THROUGH NEXT THURSDAY AT 5PM


Voting members have until next Thursday, September 4, at 5:00 p.m. to cast their ballot for candidates for the 2015 SDAR Board of Directors. If you are eligible to vote, you will have already received an e-ballot that allows you to vote online with your Member Number and a PIN code. (If you no longer have the PIN code, you will be able to recover it.)

A Candidate Guide is available to view prior to voting. Seventeen candidates are running for 10 Director positions, which are two-year terms. The election results will be posted at SDAR's main office and here on September 8. The results will be announced at the Annual Meeting on September 12 at the Sheraton Hotel and Marina on Harbor Island (prior to the annual Real Estate Summit). For event details and to register, click here.
