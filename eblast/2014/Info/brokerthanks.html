THANK YOU FOR YOUR SUPPORT
A MESSAGE FROM YOUR PRESIDENT


Dear Brokers and Office Managers,

As we approach the end of the year, I want to express my deep appreciation for the support you and your associates have given to the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® in 2013. This has been a challenging, but ultimately an exciting year of rebound for our industry. We have all been extremely busy!

We especially appreciate your teamwork in the variable dues process enforced by the California Association of REALTORS®. We are proud to be one of the associations in compliance and we could not have done it without your help and commitment.

Through all the cycles of the real estate market, SDAR has been here to reinforce the good work that you and your agents do each day to make the dream of homeownership come true. You have made SDAR your association of choice. That makes us stronger and more vital, and allows us to continue to provide tools, resources, and benefits to make your business a success.

Let's continue to work together in moving our professional accomplishments forward. Please share with us your ideas and suggestions so we can best serve your needs. Together, we can!!! Wishing you a very happy holiday season and that 2014 is filled with continued success.

Onward and upward!
Thank you,
 Linda Lee, Broker, CPA, CRS, CIPS, ePRO, GRI, PMN, RMS
2013 President of the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS®
