NOMINATIONS FOR 2015 SDAR DIRECTORS
RECOMMEND A CANDIDATE FOR THIS YEAR'S ELECTION


Take this opportunity to give back to your industry and the organization dedicated to helping you to achieve your career goals. By serving on the SDAR Board of Directors, you can help lead our organization to be the best it can be and help guide policies that will serve our members into the future.

The Nominating/Election Committee is seeking recommendations for two-year positions as an SDAR Director. If you know someone who is a volunteer that qualifies for a Director position, we encourage you to nominate them. Self-nominations are also accepted.

Nominations are due by 5:00 p.m., Monday, May 19

Click here for the Nomination Form and Requirements.

If you are not eligible this year, please get involved in supporting your association by joining an SDAR committee.
