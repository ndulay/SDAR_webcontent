PROTECT YOUR CLIENTS BY SUPPORTING PROP 13.
SDAR's Political Action Committee and The REALTOR® Party are actively working to protect Proposition 13 in California. Prop 13 protects homeowners by freezing property values at their 1975 value and restricting annual property tax increases.
