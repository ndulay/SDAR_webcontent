MEMBER ADVISORY PROGRAM
Your Voice Matters
Your Feedback is Important to Us

SDAR is looking for members to actively participate in our Member Advisory Program (MAP) - an opportunity for you to provide us a direct line of communication, and contribute insight to help us to fine tune current offerings.

As a participant of MAP, you will be invited to complete short surveys and help in beta programs that address a variety of topics ranging from how SDAR performs, to new member benefits, to sentiments about buyers, sellers and the market. Most recently, SDAR launched a new membership product, Engage121, with the input of our valued MAP members through a beta program.

Enroll in MAP today to have your voice heard!
