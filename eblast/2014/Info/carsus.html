NOTICE TO SDAR MEMBERS
C.A.R. REVIEW OF SUSPENSIONS AND EXPULSIONS


SDAR is participating in a two-year pilot program of the National Association of REALTORS® by which a suspension or expulsion that is imposed by SDAR on a member for a violation of the Code of Ethics shall be reported to the California Association of REALTORS® (C.A.R.). The violation will be reviewed by a panel of state directors, and possibly imposed on a statewide basis.

If you are suspended or expelled by SDAR and reviewed by the C.A.R. panel, you will be given an opportunity to file a written statement explaining why you believe the penalty should not be imposed statewide.
