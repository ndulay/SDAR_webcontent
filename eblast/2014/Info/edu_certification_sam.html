Protect Your Clients by Supporting Prop 13!
SDAR's Political Action Committee and The REALTOR® Party are actively working to protect Proposition 13 in California. Prop 13 protects homeowners by limiting increases in property taxes. This amendment to California's Constitution decreased property taxes by freezing property values at their 1975 value and restricting any annual property tax increases. Increases in property taxes would have dire effects on senior homeowners, many of whom live on fixed incomes. Join our fight to protect Prop 13 by donating to The REALTOR® Party!
