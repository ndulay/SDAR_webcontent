THEY HEARD OUR REALTOR® VOICE!
CITY COUNCIL RESCINDED THE JOBS TAX


Welcome to the REALTOR® Voice - Your monthly resource for political & legislative updates affecting REALTORS® in San Diego County.

The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) and the Commercial Real Estate Alliance of San Diego (CRASD) were able to defeat a 300% - 900% increase in the Linkage Fee; a direct tax on real estate, the local economy, and jobs.

By uniting to fight this unnecessary fee and tax increase, we were able to protect our local economy and local homeowners. As REALTORS®, we have an important voice in the local legislative process. We are committed to protecting the private property rights of all San Diegans while continuing to create a comprehensive plan to fund affordable housing.

Click Here to read more about our REALTOR® victory and the City Council decision to rescind the Linkage Fee.

MAYOR FAULCONER SELECTS TWO REALTORS® FOR HIS TRANSITION TEAM

Newly elected Mayor Kevin Faulconer selects SDAR's 2014 President Leslie Kilpatrick and Past President Bob Kevane to be key members of his Transition Advisory Committee. Our leaders will help ensure that a healthy Real Estate market is at the top of his agenda. SDAR's political influence is a direct result our members’ strong involvement in the political arena. As a community leader, SDAR has earned the respect of many local elected officials and cities. This enables all REALTOR® voices to be heard, and our industry and clients to be protected.

SDAR WORKING TO DEFEAT PROPOSED NEW TAXES

SDAR is currently working on solutions to enhance our community, real estate, private property rights, and REALTORS® bottom line! We still face many challenges including point-of-sale mandates, a business license tax, and sign restrictions. Please watch for future editions of the REALTOR® Voice regarding these important issues. Like the defeated Linkage Fee proposal, these issues would increase the cost of housing, delay transactions with new restrictions and requirements, and affect REALTORS®’profitability.
