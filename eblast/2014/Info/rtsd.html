VOLUNTEER TO MAKE A DIFFERENCE
JOIN THE CREW FOR REBUILDING TOGETHER THIS SATURDAY


Help us support Rebuilding Together San Diego again this year for the annual Rebuilding Day on Saturday, April 27. This year the SDAR team will help rehabilitate a Masonic Lodge/Housing Complex - a Logan Heights landmark.

All skill levels are needed for painting, cleaning, landscaping, and more. Volunteers are welcome to work all day, or on the morning or early afternoon shifts, and lunch will be provided.

Join us for this fun and rewarding day to help our San Diego neighbors.
