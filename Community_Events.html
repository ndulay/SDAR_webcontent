Raise Funds to Provide Free Transportation for Kids with Cancer

Join the Emilio Nares Foundation and Road Runner Sports to raise funds to provide free transportation to treatment centers for kids with cancer in San Diego. Grab your friends and get registered for a 5K Run/Walk. You will beam with pride knowing you're running or walking to support families whose children are battling cancer in our hometown.

Coronado Real Estate Association Sponsors Summer Concert

The Coronado Real Estate Association, a partner organization of SDAR, is sponsoring the Sunday, July 13, Coronado Promenade Summer Concert featuring a tribute to ABBA. Look for the CREA tent, bring a food item to share, and your beverage of choice. Ken Tablang's famous paella will be served. The concert is at Spreckels Park at 7th and Orange Avenue, and is free and open to the public. Concert Information.
