The 2013 Installation of SDAR’s 2013 Officers and Directors takes place on Saturday, January 12, 2013, at the Sheraton San Diego Hotel & Marina. Join incoming President Linda Lee for this elegant event that also includes the presentation of the Awards of Excellence.

Installation Dinner & Dance

What: For over 100 years San Diego’s top REALTORS®, elected officials, and dignitaries gather to celebrate the installation for its incoming leadership and preeminent leaders in the San Diego real estate industry. This famous, high profile event attracts over 500 attendees and consists of intimate networking with industry leaders, media coverage for companies and professionals, and a fantastic awards ceremony, all set within an evening of dinner, dancing, and authentic Chinese traditions.  
Who: Make a splash this coming year and stand apart as an industry leader before 500 REALTORS®.
Photos: See photos from last year's event.

President's Circle Reception

What: The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® 2013 President, Linda Lee, the National Association of REALTORS® and California Association of REALTORS® leadership, and local dignitaries gather at this President's Circle Reception to benefit REALTOR® advocacy.
Who: Network with local dignitaries, SDAR, C.A.R., and NAR leadership at this reception.

*Contributions to SDAR's Political Action Committees (PACs) are voluntary and not tax deductible for federal and state income tax purposes. Your contribution will be allocated among SDAR's two PAC's with 50% allocated to the SDAR PAC and 50% allocated to the SDAR IMPAC. The SDAR PAC is used to support candidates for public office that support REALTORS® and the SDAR IMPAC is used to support legislative and political advocacy to protect the interests of REALTORS® and to further the goals of the real estate industry.
