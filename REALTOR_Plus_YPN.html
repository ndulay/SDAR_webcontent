SDAR’s Young Professionals Network is a growing group of young, career-minded real estate professionals who want to stay abreast of the latest tools, resources, and networking opportunities. YPN will helps us reach a new and growing segment in our REALTOR® family and focus on core areas such as: Technology, networking, standards, education, community, and a mentoring program.

As a member, you’ll receive:

  • Invitations to exclusive events at REALTOR® conferences
  • News on how to get involved in leadership roles at NAR
  • Updates on new tools and resources that will help you grow your career
