As a member of the Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR), you will belong to the largest real estate association in San Diego. For more than 100 years SDAR has helped members navigate the peaks and valleys of the local real estate. That is why real estate professionals, elected officials, media and consumers have all looked to SDAR as "The Trusted Voice of San Diego Real Estate."

SDAR empowers members to be greater in all aspects of their business to do this we continually invest in resources, tools, education, programs, and services that benefit more than 12,000 members. Join SDAR today!

{mooblock=Students}

SDAR encourages you to enjoy the benefits of a FREE student membership. Your membership allows you to receive publications, statistical information, and updates about the real estate industry. Once you have obtained your real estate license and are affiliated with a broker who is a member of SDAR, your student membership will allow you to upgrade your membership to REALTOR® status for a discounted price. This membership is for individuals seeking an undergraduate or graduate degree with a specialization or major in real estate at institutions of higher learning and who have completed at least one college level course in real estate (and are currently enrolled), but are not engaged in the real estate profession on their own account nor associated with a real estate firm performing acts requiring a real estate license.
