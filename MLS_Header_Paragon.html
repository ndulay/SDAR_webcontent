As a REALTOR®, you depend on the Multiple Listing Service (MLS) for detailed, accurate and comprehensive information about all homes for sale in our region. It provides a universe of property data that just can't be found in public real estate websites, and more information that will help your clients find their perfect home.

Sandicor's trainers present live, lecture-style training on San Diego County's MLS. Monthly classes on Paragon MLS are designed not only for the novice MLS user, but also the experienced agent. Take advantage of these free courses.
