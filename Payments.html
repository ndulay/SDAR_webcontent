MLS Fees

Participation gives you access to Sandicor, San Diego's Multiple Listing Service (MLS), who provides services to licenses Real Estate Professionals.

MLS Participation Fees

Broker:
Application fee: $250
Quarterly fee: $149*
Scout Fee: $18 (one-time purchase fee for SAFE/MLS®)

Agent:
Application fee: $25
Quarterly fee: $149*
Scout Fee: $18 (one-time purchase fee for SAFE/MLS®)

Appraiser:
Application fee: $250
Quarterly fee: $149*
Scout Fee: $18 (one-time purchase fee for SAFE/MLS®)

MLS Assistant: Application fee: $25
Quarterly fee: $24*
Scout Fee: $18 (one-time purchase fee for SAFE/MLS®)

Reactivation fee for service is $28

* fee is prorated monthly.
