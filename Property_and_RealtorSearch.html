The Greater San Diego Association of REALTORS® (SDAR) has been the leading real estate organization serving San Diego County for more than 100 years. Real estate professionals and the news media rely on SDAR to give key marketplace data and provide information on new real estate laws that affect their clients.
As The Trusted Voice of San Diego Real Estate, SDAR also provides buyers and sellers with all the resources required for all their real estate needs.

  • Online Database Of Upcoming Open Houses
  • Online Database Of REALTORS®
  • Search Local And National Listings
  • International Property

Why use a REALTOR®?

REALTORS® are different than real estate agents.
As members of a local, state, and national association, they abide to a strict code of ethics.
