Cast your vote for Directors for the 2015 SDAR Board beginning Monday, August 25, at 8:00 a.m. through Thursday, September 4, at 5:00 p.m. Seventeen candidates are running for 10 Director positions, which are two-year terms. Ten other directors are currently serving their first year of a two-year term.

Eligible SDAR REALTOR® members will receive an e-ballot with voting instructions from Internet Crusade. You will be able to vote directly from the e-mail ballot, or you may vote when you visit the SDAR website or one of our SDAR Service Centers, as long as you have your PIN information from the e-mail. (Note: New members must have attended new member orientation by July 19, 2014, in order to be eligible to vote in this election.)

The election results will be posted at SDAR's main office and here on September 8. The results will be announced at the Annual Meeting on September 12 at the Sheraton Hotel and Marina on Harbor Island (prior to the annual Real Estate Summit). For event details and to register, click here.

Timeline of Election of 2015 Board of Directors

August 25 Online voting begins at 8:00 a.m. (E-ballot sent via e-mail to all eligible voters)
September 4 Online polls close at 5:00 p.m.
September 5 Nominating/Election Committee meets to receive results
September 8 Election results posted at SDAR office and on SDAR.com
September 12 Annual Meeting (President declares election results)
