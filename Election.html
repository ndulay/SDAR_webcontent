Timeline of Election of Board of Directors

July 7 Deadline to petition to be on the ballot AND in the Candidate Guide.*
July 23 Record Date (Voting members must have their current e-mail address on file at SDAR prior to this date. Contact Member Services or go online to ensure your e-mail address is current.)
July 25 Deadline to petition to be on ballot only*
August 25 Online voting begins at 8:00 a.m. (E-ballot sent via e-mail to all eligible members.)
September 4 Online polls close at 5:00 p.m.
September 5 Nominating/Election Committee meets to receive results.
September 6 Election results posted at SDAR office and on SDAR.com.
September 12 Annual Meeting (President declares election results)

* Petition Process as per SDAR Bylaws: "Additional candidates may be placed in nomination by a petition filed with an Officer of the Association at least 30 days before the election, signed by 2% of the REALTOR® members eligible to vote" (as of Record Date). Only official petitions obtained from SDAR may be used. Only original signatures will be counted. E-mail Susanne Torre at storre@sdar.com for petition form and guidelines.
